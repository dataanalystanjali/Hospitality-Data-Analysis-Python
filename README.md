# Hospitality Data Analysis using Python

## Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a hospitality booking dataset to uncover meaningful business insights. The analysis focuses on booking trends, occupancy performance, revenue generation, cancellation behavior, and customer booking patterns. The objective is to transform raw booking data into actionable insights that can support business decision-making in the hospitality industry.

---

## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are to:

* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure data quality.
* Analyze occupancy rates across different room categories.
* Evaluate booking performance across various booking platforms.
* Study revenue generation and realized revenue trends.
* Examine booking cancellations and their impact on revenue.
* Identify seasonal and monthly booking patterns.
* Analyze customer ratings and booking behavior.
* Generate business insights that can help improve occupancy, revenue, and operational efficiency.

## Key Business Insights

Some of the insights generated during the analysis include:

## Key Business Insights

1. The Presidential Suite recorded the highest occupancy percentage (58.05%) among all room categories, indicating strong demand for premium accommodations.

2. Booking Platform AtliQ Exotica generated the highest realized revenue, making it the most valuable acquisition channel.

3. City Delhi achieved the highest average occupancy percentage, highlighting stronger customer demand in that market.

4. Weekend occupancy was higher(72.34%) than weekday occupancy(50.88), suggesting increased leisure travel during weekends.

5. May recorded the highest realized revenue, indicating peak business performance during analyzed period.

6.Revenue realization was 84%, reflecting efficient booking fulfillment and low revenue leakage.

7.Mumbai generated the highest realized revenue of Rs 66.8 Crore, indicating strong market performance compared to other cities.

8.Customer ratings remainded consistent across all cities, with an average of approximately 3.5 out of 5.
